A bit of background

Every four years during the Olympics, we all love watching the swimming events. But how much does everyone really know about swimming as a sport? This page is here to tell you more about swimming and what makes it the greatest sport of all time.

Competitive swimming is made up of four different strokes, and competitions usually take place in either a 25 yd or 50 meter pool. 50 meter pools are considered Olympic sized. Most pools are 6 or 8 lanes, and all strokes with the exception of backstroke start with a dive start from a starting block.
